# Problem 04: Container With Most Water

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Topic:** Two Pointers / Greedy

## Statement

You are given an integer array `height` of size `n`. There are `n` vertical lines drawn such that the two endpoints of the `i`-th line are `(i, 0)` and `(i, height[i])`.

Find two lines that together with the x-axis form a container, such that the container contains the most water.

Return the maximum amount of water a container can store.

**Notice:** You may not slant the container.

## Examples

**Input:** `height = [1, 8, 6, 2, 5, 4, 8, 3, 7]`
**Output:** `49`
**Explanation:** The maximum amount of water is 49 (between index 1 and index 8).

**Input:** `height = [1, 1]`
**Output:** `1`

## Constraints

- `height.length == n`
- `2 <= n <= 10^5`
- `0 <= height[i] <= 10^4`
